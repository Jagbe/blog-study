How to find your artistic style

An examination of iconic pieces

It's just a movie; or show; or game; or so they say. As an artist who wishes to understand the dissemination of ideas, one must come to accept the fact that most won't see the significance in their pieces. At least not initially. Nevertheless, artistic design choices can change cultures, and have done so many times in the past. The question is how commited one is in the face of opposition.

We must speak out in all areas of culture about policy, standards, and mentalities that need to be changed. It all begins with perceptiveness, then discussion, then action, and finally change. One must seek out other innovators and the eager early adopters if they hope to ever have an impact on the masses.
